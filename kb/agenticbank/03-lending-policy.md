# Lending Policy — PSL-003

**Version:** 5.1  **Effective:** 2025-10-01  **Owner:** Credit Risk

## 1. Approval Authority Matrix
| Loan size | Approver | Conditions |
|---|---|---|
| Up to EUR 50,000 | Branch credit officer | Score >= 650 |
| EUR 50,001 – 250,000 | Regional credit committee | Score >= 700, DTI < 40% |
| EUR 250,001 – 2M | Head of Credit | Two independent appraisals |
| Above EUR 2M | Board credit committee | External rating required |

## 2. Affordability Stress Test
Apply a +300 bps interest stress on the contractual rate. DTI under stress must
remain below 45% for retail; ICR > 1.25x for SME.

## 3. Concentration Limits
No single counterparty > 10% of CET1; no single industry > 20% of book.

## 4. Prohibited Sectors
Adult content, unlicensed gambling, unregulated crypto exchanges, weapons
manufacturing without export licence, controversial extractives.
