# Customer Due Diligence (CDD) Procedure — PSL-005

**Version:** 2.0  **Effective:** 2025-12-01  **Owner:** Compliance

## 1. Standard CDD Pack
- Identification (PSL-001 §3)
- Source-of-funds declaration
- Purpose-of-account questionnaire
- Sanctions screening (PSL-011)

## 2. Enhanced DD (EDD)
Additionally:
- Source-of-wealth evidence (last 3 years)
- Adverse media check (CertiTrace adverse media feed)
- Senior compliance officer sign-off
- Annual review instead of triennial

## 3. EDD Triggers
- PEP or close associate
- High-risk jurisdiction (FATF black/grey list)
- Cash-intensive business
- Beneficial ownership > 3 layers deep
