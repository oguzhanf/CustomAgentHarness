# Know-Your-Customer (KYC) Policy — PSL-001

**Version:** 4.2  **Effective:** 2025-09-01  **Owner:** Compliance

## 1. Purpose
Establish identification and verification standards AgenticBank applies to every
customer before account opening and on an ongoing basis, in line with applicable
AML regulations.

## 2. Customer Categories & Thresholds
| Category | Initial CDD | Enhanced DD trigger |
|---|---|---|
| Retail individual (low risk) | Standard | Single transfer > EUR 15,000 |
| Retail individual (high risk) | Enhanced | Any cross-border transfer |
| Corporate (SME) | Enhanced | Annual turnover > EUR 5M |
| Corporate (large) | Enhanced + senior approval | Any politically-exposed beneficial owner |
| PEP | Enhanced + senior approval + ongoing review | Always |

**High-risk threshold for unattended account opening: EUR 15,000.** Above this
threshold a human relationship manager must perform a video KYC interview.

## 3. Required Documents
- Government photo ID
- Proof of address < 90 days old
- For corporate: certificate of incorporation, UBO declaration, board resolution

## 4. Ongoing Monitoring
- Transaction monitoring rules engine reviews 100% of transactions
- Annual customer-risk-rating refresh
- Trigger-based reviews: address change, sudden volume increase > 300% MoM
