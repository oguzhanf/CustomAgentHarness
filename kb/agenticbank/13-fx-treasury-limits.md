# FX & Treasury Limits — PSL-013

**Version:** 6.0  **Effective:** 2026-01-02  **Owner:** Treasury

## 1. Overnight FX Open Position Limit
Aggregate: EUR 25M equivalent. Single currency: EUR 5M (G10), EUR 1M (EM).

## 2. Counterparty Lines
| Counterparty band | Settlement limit | Pre-settlement limit |
|---|---|---|
| AAA / AA | EUR 200M | EUR 50M |
| A | EUR 100M | EUR 25M |
| BBB | EUR 25M | EUR 5M |
| Below BBB | Not permitted | Not permitted |

## 3. Stop-loss
Trader-level daily stop: EUR 200k. Desk-level monthly stop: EUR 1M.
