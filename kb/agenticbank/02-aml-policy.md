# Anti-Money-Laundering (AML) Standard — PSL-002

**Version:** 3.7  **Effective:** 2025-07-15  **Owner:** Compliance

## 1. Scope
All business lines and entities of AgenticBank Group.

## 2. Customer Risk Rating
A four-tier model: Low / Medium / High / Prohibited. Drivers include
geography (FATF list), product, channel, occupation, and beneficial owner
structure.

## 3. Suspicious Activity Reporting
Front-line staff must file an internal SAR within 24h of suspicion. The MLRO
reviews and escalates externally within 5 business days where warranted.

## 4. Transaction Monitoring Rules (selected)
| Rule ID | Description | Action |
|---|---|---|
| TM-014 | Structured cash deposits aggregating to > EUR 9,500 in 7 days | Hold + investigate |
| TM-027 | Cross-border wire to non-cooperative jurisdiction | Block + escalate |
| TM-041 | Velocity > 30 incoming transfers/day on retail account | Alert + RM review |

## 5. Training
All staff complete annual AML refresher; front-line bankers complete additional
typology training quarterly.
