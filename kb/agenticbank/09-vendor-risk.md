# Vendor Risk Management — PSL-009

**Version:** 2.1  **Effective:** 2025-11-12  **Owner:** Procurement / Tech Risk

## 1. Tiering
| Tier | Criteria | Assessment cadence |
|---|---|---|
| Critical | Hosts customer data OR supports a critical business function | Annual + on material change |
| Significant | Hosts internal sensitive data | Biennial |
| Standard | All other | Triennial |

## 2. AI-specific Diligence
For any vendor providing AI services or AI-embedded products:
- Disclose model provenance and training data classes
- Provide responsible-AI assessment
- Describe content-filtering and grounding mechanisms
- Allow audit of telemetry pipeline
- Confirm support for sensitive-information protection (e.g. Purview)
