# Data Privacy Standard — PSL-008

**Version:** 3.0  **Effective:** 2025-05-25  **Owner:** Data Privacy Office

## 1. Lawful Basis Register
Maintained by the DPO; reviewed when a new processing activity is introduced.

## 2. Data Subject Rights
| Right | Service-level target |
|---|---|
| Access | 30 calendar days |
| Rectification | 30 calendar days |
| Erasure (where applicable) | 30 calendar days |
| Portability | 30 calendar days |
| Objection / restriction | 7 calendar days |

## 3. Cross-border Transfers
Adequacy decision preferred. Where not available: SCCs + transfer impact
assessment + supplementary measures.

## 4. AI & Personal Data
Any AI tool processing personal data must be entered in the RoPA. Use of
generative AI for customer correspondence requires explicit consent unless the
content is purely operational (e.g. transaction notification).
