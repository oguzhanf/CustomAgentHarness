# Mortgage Origination Standard — PSL-012

**Version:** 3.3  **Effective:** 2026-02-01  **Owner:** Retail Credit

## 1. Maximum LTV by Property Type
| Property | Max LTV |
|---|---|
| Owner-occupied primary | 80% (90% with mortgage insurance) |
| Second home | 70% |
| Buy-to-let | 65% |
| New construction (off-plan) | 60% with staged drawdown |

## 2. Income Multiples
Cap at 4.5x gross household income, subject to PSL-003 affordability stress.

## 3. Valuation
Independent surveyor required. AVM acceptable only for LTV < 60% on prime
metropolitan addresses.
