# High-Risk Customer Procedure — PSL-006

**Version:** 1.4  **Effective:** 2026-02-20  **Owner:** Compliance

## 1. Definition
A customer flagged as **High** in the AML rating engine (PSL-002 §2) OR meeting
any single EDD trigger (PSL-005 §3).

## 2. Onboarding
- Must be approved by a Regional Compliance Officer
- Video KYC interview required, recorded and retained 7 years
- Account flagged in core banking with red-band UI to all servicing staff

## 3. Ongoing
- Transaction monitoring threshold lowered 30% across all rules
- Annual EDD refresh
- Quarterly review by MLRO

## 4. Exit
If risk cannot be mitigated, exit must complete within 90 days. Phased exit
permitted only with MLRO + Head of Compliance sign-off.
