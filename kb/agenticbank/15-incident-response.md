# Incident Response Playbook — PSL-015

**Version:** 2.2  **Effective:** 2026-04-15  **Owner:** CISO

## 1. Severity Classification
| Sev | Definition | Response time |
|---|---|---|
| Sev1 | Confirmed data breach or live attack | Immediate; CIRT lead paged |
| Sev2 | High-confidence attempt detected | 30 min |
| Sev3 | Anomaly under investigation | 4h |
| Sev4 | Informational | Next business day |

## 2. Communications
Sev1: regulator notified within 24h, customers within 72h where required.
PR-approved templates only.

## 3. AI Incident Class
A new sub-category as of v2.2: incidents arising from AI agents misbehaving,
including unintended data egress, prompt injection compromise, hallucinated
recommendations causing harm. Triage owner: CISO with Tech Risk co-lead.
